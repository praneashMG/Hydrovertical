Frequently Asked Questions

🌱 How do I get started with vertical farming?
Schedule a free consultation with our team. We'll assess your space, budget, and goals to design a custom solution. Most farms are operational within 60-90 days.
💰 What is the typical ROI for your systems?
Our clients typically see ROI within 14-24 months. Commercial operations achieve 45-60% gross margins with payback periods varying based on scale and crop selection.
🚚 Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! We ship to 28+ countries worldwide. Our logistics team handles customs clearance and provides on-site installation support for international clients.
🛠️ What kind of support do you provide?
24/7 technical support, remote monitoring, quarterly performance reviews, software updates, and annual on-site maintenance. Plus, you get a dedicated account manager.
📦 How to become an authorized supplier?
Visit our supplier portal at suppliers.hvfs.com. Complete the application with your certifications and product catalog. Our procurement team reviews within 2-3 business days.
🔧 Do you offer training for my team?
Absolutely! We provide comprehensive on-site training, certification programs, and ongoing educational resources. Our training center in Oregon hosts monthly workshops.
