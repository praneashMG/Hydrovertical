Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about vertical hydroponic farming — from setup to harvest.

What exactly is hydroponic vertical farming?

Hydroponic vertical farming is a method of growing plants without soil, using nutrient-rich water solutions in vertically stacked layers. This system maximizes space efficiency, reduces water usage by up to 90%, and allows year-round cultivation in controlled environments — ideal for urban areas and locations with poor soil quality.

How much space do I need to start a vertical farm?

Our systems are highly scalable. Home growers can start with as little as 20-50 sq ft (a corner of a room or balcony). Commercial operations typically begin with 200-500 sq ft, but we've successfully deployed farms in spaces as small as 100 sq ft and as large as 10,000+ sq ft warehouses. We offer free space assessment to recommend the optimal configuration.

What types of crops can I grow with your systems?

Our vertical hydroponic systems support a wide variety of crops including leafy greens (lettuce, kale, spinach, arugula), herbs (basil, mint, cilantro, parsley), microgreens, strawberries, and even fruiting plants like cherry tomatoes and peppers. Each system is customizable based on your target crops, with adjustable lighting spectrums and nutrient formulations.

How does the AI monitoring system work?

Our proprietary AI platform uses IoT sensors to track 30+ parameters including pH, EC (nutrient concentration), temperature, humidity, CO₂ levels, and light intensity. The system automatically adjusts nutrient dosing, lighting schedules, and climate controls in real-time. You can monitor everything remotely via our mobile dashboard, receive harvest predictions, and get proactive maintenance alerts.

Are the LED grow lights energy efficient?

Yes — our full-spectrum LED arrays consume up to 40% less energy than traditional grow lights while delivering optimal light wavelengths for each growth stage. They feature programmable schedules, dimming capabilities, and heat recovery systems that can reduce overall energy costs by 30-35% compared to conventional lighting solutions.

How long does installation take from order to operation?

For home systems, delivery takes 5-7 business days with 2-4 hours of self-installation using our video guides. Commercial systems typically require 2-4 weeks for custom manufacturing, followed by 3-7 days of on-site installation by our certified technicians. We also offer remote installation support and training to minimize downtime.

Do you provide international shipping and support?

Absolutely. We ship to over 35 countries worldwide with full export documentation, customs clearance assistance, and real-time tracking. Our global support team provides 24/7 technical assistance, and we have regional partners in North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, and the Middle East to ensure quick response times.

How much daily/weekly maintenance is required?

Thanks to our automation, daily tasks take only 10-15 minutes — mostly checking water levels and visual crop inspection. Weekly tasks include cleaning filters, calibrating sensors (5 minutes), and nutrient top-ups. Our AI system sends alerts when action is needed, so you never miss important maintenance. Commercial systems include optional automated cleaning cycles.

What is the lifespan of the equipment?

Our vertical towers and NFT channels are made from food-grade, UV-stabilized materials rated for 10+ years of continuous use. LED grow lights last 50,000+ hours (approximately 8-10 years of 16-hour daily cycles). Pumps have a 5-year warranty, and sensors typically last 3-5 years with proper maintenance. All components are modular and replaceable.

What is the typical return on investment (ROI) for commercial farms?

Based on data from our 500+ commercial installations, the average payback period is 14-24 months. Gross margins typically range from 45-70% for leafy greens and herbs, depending on local market prices and operational efficiency. Factors affecting ROI include crop selection, local electricity costs, and distribution channels. We provide detailed financial modeling during consultation.

Do you offer financing or leasing options?

Yes — we partner with agricultural lenders to offer flexible financing including equipment leasing, revenue-share agreements, and 0% interest installment plans for qualified commercial projects. Home systems can be purchased via installment payments through our checkout process. Contact our sales team for a customized financing proposal based on your credit and business plan.

What support do you provide after purchase?

All systems include 12 months of premium support with 24/7 technical assistance, remote diagnostics, and quarterly performance reviews. We also offer extended support plans with on-site visits, priority response (under 2 hours), and access to our agronomy team for crop optimization. Plus, lifetime software updates and a knowledge base with video tutorials.
