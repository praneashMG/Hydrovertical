About Our Company

Engineering a resilient food future — one vertical tower at a time.

OUR ROOTS

From a single lab to a global movement

HydroNex was born in 2019 inside a modest university greenhouse — a collaboration between agronomists and software engineers who believed that fresh, nutritious produce should thrive even in dense urban cores. Facing climate uncertainties and soil degradation, we set out to build vertical farming ecosystems that are scalable, autonomous, and regenerative.

Today, our systems operate across four continents, empowering communities to grow hyper-local food with 90% less water and zero chemical runoff. Our vision remains unchanged: democratize access to controlled-environment agriculture.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make year‑round, pesticide‑free farming accessible to every community by deploying intelligent vertical hydroponic systems that respect planetary boundaries.

Our Vision

A world where fresh produce travels meters, not miles — where every rooftop, basement, and vacant lot becomes a thriving farm powered by AI and nature.
